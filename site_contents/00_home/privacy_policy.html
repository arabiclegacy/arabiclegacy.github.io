Arabic Legacy is a personal website that doesn't aim to earn any financial profit. It is basically a blogging website. Therefore, it is putting great significance to protect your private sphere as a user.
Basically, we don't do any processing of personal data. You do not need to reveal personal data to use our website.

Collecting and Processing -
We don't collect personal data. However for counting statistics only, we may need to log information about

  • Which webpages have been accessed
  • Which documentations have been downloaded by the user who accessed our webpages
  • Date and time of access or download of our webpages or documentations
  • IP of the user who accessed our webpages or downloaded anything
  • Type of Webbrowser and Operating System used during access of our webpages

This logging is carried out for internal statistical purposes. There is no legal obligation under the Federal Data Protection Act to employ a data-protection officer. As a concerned party, you have the right to contact the data-protection supervisory authorities as necessary. A list of supervisory authorities as well as their contact data can be obtained at the following link:
https://www.bfdi.bund.de/DE/Infothek/Anschriften_Links/anschriften_links-node.html.

Usage and sharing -
Since we don't collect personal data, Users of Arabic Legacy website are anonymous. We get previous mentioned statistical data from statistics logging tool. We don't share, rent or sell any of these statistics with third parties. Exception is in the case that these statistics are asked from governmental institutions and agencies as required by law.

Rights to information -
Since we don't collect personal data, we can't provide feedback about logged statistics to our users. This is because users are anonymous for us.
